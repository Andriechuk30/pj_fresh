Fresh Property Group (FPG) is a leading expert in the purpose built accommodation sector with an unrivalled track record, coverage and scale.

The company turns great buildings into great places to live by offering a single source solution for clients in the student and build to rent sectors, through its Fresh Student Living and Five Nine Living brands.

The group acts as the backbone to both service lines, providing national infrastructure and ensuring they can act responsively to the intricate nuances and requirements of each market segment.

What We Do

We help investors and developers maximise the value of their assets by providing high quality, great value purpose built accommodation management that achieves the highest levels of occupancy and strong revenue streams for our clients.
